# **_🚗 BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke)_**

> *"The Ultimate Driving Machine"* — BMW's long-standing brand slogan emphasizing performance, innovation, and driving pleasure.

## **_Overview_**

**BMW** (*Bayerische Motoren Werke AG*) is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Founded in **1916**, BMW is known worldwide for its premium automobiles, advanced engineering, and performance-oriented driving experience.

### **_Key Facts_**

| Feature | Details |
|----------|----------|
| Company Name | BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) |
| Founded | 1916 |
| Headquarters | Munich, Germany |
| Industry | Automotive & Motorcycles |
| Founder | Franz Josef Popp, Karl Rapp, and Camillo Castiglioni |
| Famous Brands | BMW, MINI, Rolls-Royce Motor Cars |
| Slogan | "The Ultimate Driving Machine" |
